Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Drip ("we", "our", "the app") is a weather application developed by an independent developer based in France. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data We Collect

Location

  • What: Your GPS position is used to display local weather
  • Storage: Your location stays on your device only
  • Sharing: Sent to Apple WeatherKit to fetch weather data
  • We do not store your location on our servers

Push Notifications

  • Service used: Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)
  • Purpose: Alert you before weather changes
  • Data: An anonymous device identifier (token)
  • You can disable notifications in settings

In-App Purchases

  • Managed by: Apple via the App Store
  • We never have access to your payment information
  • Apple handles: payments, refunds, renewals

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Name, email, or personal information
  • User accounts
  • Browsing history
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Payment information

Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with:

Service Data Purpose
Apple WeatherKit GPS location Fetch weather data
Firebase (Google) Device token Push notifications

We never sell your data.

Security

  • All communications are encrypted (HTTPS)
  • Data primarily stays on your device
  • No user database on our servers

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you can:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data (uninstall the app)
  • Disable location or notifications in iOS settings

Children

The app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The last updated date will be changed accordingly.
