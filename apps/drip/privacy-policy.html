Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Drip ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Location Data

Drip requires access to your device's location to provide accurate weather information for your current position. We use your location data solely to:

  • Fetch weather forecasts from Apple WeatherKit
  • Display your current city and neighborhood

Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

Weather Data

We use Apple WeatherKit to retrieve weather information. Weather requests are made directly to Apple's servers. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles this data.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App. This data does not personally identify you and may include:

  • App crashes and error reports
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device type and iOS version

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Deliver accurate weather forecasts based on your location
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Respond to your requests or questions

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Drip processes most data locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data that is transmitted or stored.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable location access in your device settings at any time
  • Delete the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Contact us to request information about your data

Children's Privacy

Drip is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
